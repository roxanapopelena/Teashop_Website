General Store

The general store available here at Chollerton Tearooms was built in 1842,
as were many mercantileshops of the time. It was a place for town residents
to purchase many of the day to day staples needed for everyday life, as well as
a place to congregate and discuss many aspects of living in the mid-19th century.
So if you are ever short on groceries or basic items, the general
store available here at Chollerton Tearooms has got your back.
